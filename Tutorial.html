###################### GROUP BY & HAVING ########################

  • select column_name1, column_name2 , column_name3 from table_name where condition Group By column_name1, column_name2....; (it can never have the same row)
  • The HAVING clause was added to SQL because the WHERE keyword cannot be used with aggregate functions
  • select col_name1, avg(col_name2) as provide_column_name from table_name group by col_name1
  • select dept , avg(salary) as avg_salary from employees group by dept having avg(salary)>1000
  • select city , sum(salary) as total from table_name group by city having sum(salary)>2000
